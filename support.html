Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a load?

Tap the Add tab at the bottom of the screen, then tap "Add a Load." Enter the broker, route, miles, and rate — revenue, rate per mile, and profit are calculated automatically as you type. Auto-scan from a photo of the rate con is a planned future feature.

How do I create a paystub manually?

Go to the More tab, then tap "Create Paystub Manually." Enter your loads, expenses, and fees. Each fee can be toggled between percentage (%) and flat dollar ($) amounts. When finished, tap "Generate PDF" to export a branded paystub.

How do I add a driver?

Go to the More tab, then tap "Drivers." Tap the + button to add a new driver. You can set each driver to either percentage-of-profit pay or a flat rate per load.

How do I change my subscription plan?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there. You can also access this from the "Plan & Billing" section in the app's Settings.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. We use Supabase with row-level security, meaning only you can access your data. You can also enable PIN lock or Face ID for additional protection.

Can I use the app offline?

Core features like manual paystub creation and settlement calculations work offline. Cloud sync requires an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at demarquishinton@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request." Include the email associated with your account. We will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
